## The Waste and Contaminated Land (Amendment) (2011 Act) (Commencement No.3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Environmental Protection

  Made   27th October 2022

  Coming into force   2nd January 2023

      The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs , in
exercise of the powers conferred by sections 12(1) of the Waste and
Contaminated Land (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2011 , makes the
following Order:

### Citation

1

This Order may be cited as the Waste and Contaminated Land (Amendment) (2011
Act) (Commencement No.3) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022.

### Appointed Day

2

The appointed day for the coming into operation of –

2.a

Section 4 (powers to require removal of waste unlawfully deposited); and

2.b

Section 5 (councils to enforce Articles 4 and 5 of 1997 Order), of the Waste
and Contaminated land (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2011 is 2nd January
2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Agriculture,
Environment and Rural Affairs on 27th October 2022.         John Mills   A
senior officer of the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This order brings into operation sections 4 and 5 of the Waste and
Contaminated Land (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2011 on 2nd January 2023.

    (This note is not part of the Order)
