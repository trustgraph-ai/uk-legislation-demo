## The Renting Homes (Amendment) (Wales) Act 2021 (Commencement) Order 2022

    Housing, Wales

  Made   12 August 2022

      The Welsh Ministers make the following Order in exercise of the power
conferred on them by section 19(2) of the Renting Homes (Amendment) (Wales)
Act 2021 .

### Title

1

The title of this Order is the Renting Homes (Amendment) (Wales) Act 2021
(Commencement) Order 2022.

### Appointed day

2

1 December 2022 is the appointed day for the coming into force of paragraph 28
of Schedule 6 to the Renting Homes (Amendment) (Wales) Act 2021.

      Julie James   Minister for Climate Change, one of the Welsh Ministers

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order is the only commencement order made by the Welsh Ministers under
the Renting Homes (Amendment) (Wales) Act 2021 (asc 3) (“ the Act ”) and it
brings paragraph 28 of Schedule 6 to the Act into force.

  The Act makes provision about security of occupation under the Renting Homes
(Wales) Act 2016 (anaw 1), miscellaneous provisions relating to occupation
contracts and other connected provisions.

  Sections 15, 17, 19 and 20 of the Act came into force on 8 April 2021 (the
day after the day on which the Act received Royal Assent) in accordance with
section 19(1) of the Act. The remaining provisions of the Act, other than
paragraph 28 of Schedule 6, came into force on 7 June 2021 (two months after
the day on which the Act received Royal Assent) in accordance with section
19(2) and (3) of the Act.

  Article 2 of this Order commences paragraph 28 of Schedule 6 to the Act on 1
December 2022.

  Paragraph 28 of Schedule 6 to the Act amends the Renting Homes (Fees etc.)
(Wales) Act 2019 (anaw 2) (“ the 2019 Act ”) so as to—     a     omit
paragraph (b) of section 6 (application of sections 2 to 5 of the 2019 Act to
pre-existing requirements and contracts), and         b     omit section 25
(power to make transitional provision in respect of assured tenancies).
