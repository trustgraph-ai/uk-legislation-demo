## The Direct Payments to Farmers (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Agriculture

  Made   1st July 2022

  Coming into force   1st August 2022

      The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs makes these
Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 48 of, and
paragraph 5 of Schedule 6 to, the Agriculture Act 2020 ,

### Citation, commencement and interpretation

1

1.1

These Regulations may be cited as The Direct Payments to Farmers (Amendment)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022 and come into operation on 1st August
2022.

1.2

In these Regulations “ Regulation (EU) 1306/2013 ” means  Regulation (EU) No.
1306/2013  of the European Parliament and of the Council on the financing,
management and monitoring of the common agricultural policy .

1.3

The Interpretation Act (Northern Ireland) 1954  shall apply to these
Regulations as it applies to an Act of the Assembly.

### Amendment of Article 75 of Regulation (EU) 1306/2013

2

Article 75 of Regulation (EU) 1306/2013 is amended as follows –

2.a

in paragraph 1(a) substitute  “but not before 16 October”  with “in any claim
year”, and

2.b

in paragraph 2 substitute  “the verification of eligibility conditions, to be
carried out by the relevant authority pursuant to Article 74, has been
finalised”  with “the administrative checks to be carried out by the relevant
authority pursuant to Article 74, have been finalised.”

    Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Agriculture,

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

  (This note is not part of the Regulations)

These Regulations amend  Regulation (EU) No. 1306/2013  to enable the
Department to make payments to beneficiaries after administrative checks.

Regulation 2 enables payments to be made before 16 October following the
completion of administrative checks only.

A full impact assessment has not been produced for this instrument as no, or
no significant, impact on the private, voluntary or public sector is foreseen.
