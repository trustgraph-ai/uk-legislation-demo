Title: Fair Employment (School Teachers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022
Date: 2022-05-13
Description: Remove the exception for school teachers from the Fair Employment and Treatment (Northern Ireland) Order 1998; and for connected purposes.
Publisher: Government Printer for Northern Ireland
Identifier: http://www.legislation.gov.uk/nia/2022/23/enacted

## Fair Employment (School Teachers) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022

      BE IT ENACTED by being passed by the Northern Ireland Assembly and
assented to by Her Majesty as follows:

### Revocation of Article 71

1

In the Fair Employment and Treatment (Northern Ireland) Order 1998 (“ the 1998
Order ”), omit Article 71 (school teachers).

### Consequential amendments

2

The Schedule to this Act has effect.

### Commencement

3

3.1

The preceding provisions of this Act come into operation on such day as the
Executive Office may by order appoint (but this is subject to subsection (2)).

3.2

Those provisions come into operation at the end of the period of 2 years
beginning with the day of Royal Assent if by the end of that period no order
has been made under subsection (1) bringing them into operation.

3.3

An order under subsection (1) may include such transitional, transitory or
saving provision as the Executive Office considers appropriate.

### Short title

4

This Act may be cited as the Fair Employment (School Teachers) Act (Northern
Ireland) 2022.
