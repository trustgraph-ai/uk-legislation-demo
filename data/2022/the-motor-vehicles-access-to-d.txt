## The Motor Vehicles (Access to Driver Licensing Records) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Road Traffic And Vehicles

  Made   1st November 2022

  Coming into operation   1st December 2022

      The Department for Infrastructure   makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by Articles 19I and 218 of the Road Traffic
(Northern Ireland) Order 1981  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Motor Vehicles (Access to Driver
Licensing Records) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into
operation on 1 st  December 2022.

### Interpretation

2

In these Regulations “ the 1981 Order ” means Road Traffic (Northern Ireland)
Order 1981.

### Access to driver licensing information

3

3.1

The Department for Infrastructure may make information held by it for the
purposes of Part 2 of the 1981 Order available to persons employed by the
authorities of any country or territory outside the United Kingdom with
responsibility under the law of that country or territory for the regulation
of drivers or vehicles, provided that country or territory has entered into an
agreement with the United Kingdom for the sharing of such information or has
been designated for the purpose of licence exchange under Article 19D(2) of
the 1981 Order

3.2

The purpose for which persons specified in paragraph (1) may be given access
to information made available to them under that paragraph is the discharge of
responsibilities for the regulation of drivers or vehicles.

3.3

The purposes for which persons may be given access to information under the
Motor Vehicles (Access to Driver Licensing Records) Regulations (Northern
Ireland) 2011  do not apply to persons specified in paragraph (1).

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  Article 19I of the Road Traffic (Northern Ireland) Order 1981 makes
provision for access to driver licensing information. These Regulations will
further allow for the sharing of driver licensing information with each
country or territory that the United Kingdom has entered into an agreement for
the sharing of such information and also those countries or territories which
have been designated for the purpose of exchanging driver licences. The
Regulations also determine that the purpose of providing access to driver
licensing information is restricted to the discharge of responsibilities for
the regulation of drivers or vehicles.
