## The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022

  Order laid before the Assembly under section 96(2) of the Coronavirus Act
2020, for approval by resolution of the Assembly before the expiration of 40
days beginning with the day on which the Order is made

    Public Health

  Made   22nd September 2022

  Laid before the Assembly   22nd September 2022

  Coming into operation   24th September 2022

      The Department of Health , in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 90(2) of the Coronavirus Act 2020 , makes the following order.

### Citation and commencement

1

This order may be cited as The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to
Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No.2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022
and shall come into operation on 24th September 2022.

### Alteration of expiry date

2

Section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the Coronavirus Act 2020 do not expire at
the time when they would otherwise expire and expire instead on 24th March
2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Health on 22nd
September 2022       (L.S.)   Robin Swann MLA   Minister of Health

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the order)

  This order extends the expiry date of section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the
Coronavirus Act 2020 to 24th March 2023.
