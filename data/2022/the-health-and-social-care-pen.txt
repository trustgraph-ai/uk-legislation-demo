Title: The Health and Social Care Pensions (Abatement) (No 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022
Date: 2023-01-27
Description: These Regulations remove the abatement provisions of the Health and Personal Social Services (Superannuation) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1995 (S.R. 1995 No.95) (“the 1995 Regulations”), the Health and Social Care (Pension Scheme) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2008 (S.R. 2008 No.256) (“the 2008 Regulations”), the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015 (S.R. 2015 No.120) (“the 2015 Regulations”) for specified periods of time.
Publisher: Statute Law Database

## The Health and Social Care Pensions (Abatement) (No 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Health And Personal Social Services   Public Service Pensions

  Made   10 October 2022

  Coming into operation   1 November 2022

      The Department of Health makes the following Regulations in exercise of
the powers conferred by Articles 12(1,) (2) and (3) and 14(1) and (2) of, and
Schedule 3 to, the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order 1972  and sections
1(1) and (2)(e), 3(1), (2) and (3) and 18(5) of, and paragraph 5 of Schedule 2
and Schedule 3 to, the Public Service Pensions Act (Northern Ireland) 2014 .
In accordance with Article 12(4) of the Superannuation (Northern Ireland)
Order 1972 and section 21(1) of the Public Service Pensions Act (Northern
Ireland) 2014, the Department has consulted the representatives of such
persons as appear to the Department likely to be affected by these
Regulations.           In accordance with Article 12(1) of the Superannuation
(Northern Ireland) Order 1972 and section 3(5) of the Public Service Pensions
Act (Northern Ireland) 2014, these Regulations are made with the consent of
the Department of Finance.

### Introductory

#### Citation and commencement

1

1.1

These Regulations may be cited as the Health and Social Care Pensions
(Abatement) (No 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022.

1.2

These Regulations come into operation on 1 November 2022.

### Amendment of the Health and Personal Social Services (Superannuation) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1995

#### General

2

The Health and Personal Social Services (Superannuation) Regulations (Northern
Ireland) 1995 are amended as provided by regulations 3 and 4.

#### Amendment to regulations 84

3

These Regulations have effect as if, for the period 1 November 2022 to 31
March 2024, (both dates inclusive) regulation 84 (suspension of pension on
return to HPSS employment) were omitted.

#### Amendment to regulations 85

4

These Regulations have effect for the period 1 November 2022 to 31 March 2025
(both dates inclusive) as if in the opening words of regulation 85(3)
(reduction of pension on return to HPSS employment) the words “or (c)” were
omitted.

### Amendments to the Health and Social Care (Pension Scheme) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2008

#### General

5

The Health and Social Care (Pension Scheme) Regulations (Northern Ireland)
2008  are amended as provided by regulation 6.

#### Amendment to regulations 50 and 181

6

These Regulations have effect as if for the period 1 November 2022 to 31 March
2023 ( both dates inclusive) –

6.a

in regulation 50 (abatement of pension following increase in pensionable pay)
paragraph (2) (a) were omitted; and

6.b

in regulation 181 (abatement of pension following increase in engagement in
employment) paragraph (2) (a) were omitted.

### Amendments to the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015

#### General

7

The Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015b
are amended as provided by regulation 8.

#### Amendment to regulation 85

8

These Regulations have effect for the period 1 November 2022 to 31 March 2023
(both dates inclusive) as if in regulation 85 (abatement of pension following
continuation of employment) paragraph (3) were omitted.

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations remove the abatement provisions of the Health and Personal
Social Services (Superannuation) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1995 ( S.R.
1995 No.95 ) (“ the 1995 Regulations ”), the Health and Social Care (Pension
Scheme) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2008 ( S.R. 2008 No.256 ) (“ the 2008
Regulations ”), the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 2015 ( S.R. 2015 No.120 ) (“ the 2015 Regulations ”) for
specified periods of time.
