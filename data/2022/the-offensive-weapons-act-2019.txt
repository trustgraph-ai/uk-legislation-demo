## The Offensive Weapons Act 2019 (Commencement No. 1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Criminal Law

  Made   29th September 2022

  Coming into operation   6th October 2022

      The Department of Justice, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 70(3) of the Offensive Weapons Act 2019  makes the following Order.

### Citation and interpretation

1

1.1

This Order may be cited as the Offensive Weapons Act 2019 (Commencement No. 1)
Order (Northern Ireland) 2022.

1.2

In this Order “ the 2019 Act ” means the Offensive Weapons Act 2019”

### Appointed Day

2

The day appointed for the coming into operation of section 6 of the 2019 Act
is 6th October 2022.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 29th
September 2022.         Naomi Long   Minister of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  This Order brings into operation on 6th October 2022 section 6 of the Act,
which is the offence of having a corrosive substance in a public place.

  For the purposes of this Order corrosive substance means a substance which
is capable of burning human skin by corrosion and public place includes any
place to which, at the time in question, the public have or are permitted
access, whether on payment or otherwise.
