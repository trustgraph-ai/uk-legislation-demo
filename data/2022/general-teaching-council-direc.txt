## General Teaching Council (Directions) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022

      BE IT ENACTED by being passed by the Northern Ireland Assembly and
assented to by Her Majesty as follows:

### Power of Department to give directions

1

1.1

The Education and Libraries (Northern Ireland) Order 1986 is amended as
follows.

1.2

In Article 101 (power of Department to give directions), after sub-paragraph
(f) of paragraph (3), insert—

1.2.fa

the General Teaching Council for Northern Ireland;

.

### Commencement and short title

2

2.1

This Act comes into operation on the day after receiving Royal Assent.

2.2

This Act may be cited as the General Teaching Council (Directions) Act
(Northern Ireland) 2022.
