## The County Court (Amendment) Rules (Northern Ireland) 2022

    County Courts   Procedure

  Made   20th September 2022

  Coming into operation -   3rd October 2022

      The County Court Rules Committee makes and the Department of Justice,
after consultation with the Lady Chief Justice, allows the following Rules in
exercise of the powers conferred by Article 47 of the County Courts (Northern
Ireland) Order 1980 .

### Citation and Commencement

1

These Rules may be cited as the County Court (Amendment) Rules (Northern
Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 3rd October 2022.

### Amendments to the County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981

2

The County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981  are amended as follows—

2.1

In Order 26, rule 3(1) for “£3,000” substitute  “£5,000” .

2.2

In Order 55, rule 19—

2.2.a

in paragraph (1)(b) for “£3,000” substitute  “£5,000” ;

2.2.b

in paragraph (2) for “£3,000” substitute  “£5,000” .

2.3

In Appendix 1, in Form 10A for “£3,000” substitute  “£5,000” .

### Saving

3

Nothing in these Rules shall affect any proceedings which are pending
immediately before these Rules come into operation and the Rules in operation
before that date shall continue to apply to those proceedings.

      P Smyth HHJ   S Crawford HHJ   A Devlin HHJ                 In exercise

Minister of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Rules)

  These Rules amend the County Court Rules (Northern Ireland) 1981 ( S.R. 1981
No. 225 ) to take account of the increase in the small claims court
jurisdiction from £3,000 to £5,000.
