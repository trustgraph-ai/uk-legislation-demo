## The Damages for Bereavement (Variation of Sum) Order (Northern Ireland) 2022

    Damages

  Made   6th October 2022

  Coming into operation   1st November 2022

      The Department of Justice makes the following Order in exercise of the
powers conferred by Article 3A(5) of the Fatal Accidents (Northern Ireland)
Order 1977  and now vested in the Department of Justice .

### Citation and commencement

1

1.1

This Order may be cited as the Damages for Bereavement (Variation of Sum)
Order (Northern Ireland) 2022 and shall come into operation on 1st November
2022.

1.2

This Order applies only to causes of action which accrue on or after 1st
November 2022.

### Variation of sum

2

In Article 3A(3)  of the Fatal Accidents (Northern Ireland) Order 1977, for
“£15,100” substitute  “£17,200” .

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 6th
October 2022         Naomi R Long   Minister of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order, which applies to causes of action accruing on or after 1st
November 2022, amends Article 3A(3) of the Fatal Accidents (Northern Ireland)
Order 1977 ( S.I. 1977/1251 (N.I. 18) ) increasing the sum which may be
awarded for damages for bereavement in Northern Ireland from £15,100 to
£17,200.
