## Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court of Session 1994 Amendment) (Court Sittings) 2022

Court Of Session

Made 17th August 2022

Laid before the Scottish Parliament 19th August 2022

Coming into force in accordance with paragraph 1(2) and (3)

The Court of Session makes this Act of Sederunt under the powers conferred by
section 103(1) of the Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014  and all other powers
enabling it to do so.

### Citation and commencement, etc.

1

1.1

This Act of Sederunt may be cited as the Act of Sederunt (Rules of the Court
of Session 1994 Amendment) (Court Sittings) 2022.

1.2

Subject to sub-paragraph (3), it comes into force on 1st January 2024.

1.3

Paragraph 2(2)(b) and schedule 2 come into force on 6th January 2024.

1.4

A certified copy is to be inserted in the Books of Sederunt.

### Amendment of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994

2

2.1

Chapter 10 (court sittings) of the Rules of the Court of Session 1994  is
amended in accordance with this paragraph.

2.2

In rule 10.3 (holiday dates, periods and vacation sitting days) —

2.2.a

in the definition of “public holiday”, for the Table substitute the Table in
schedule 1;

2.2.b

in the definition of “vacation”, for the Table substitute the Table in
schedule 2;

2.2.c

in the definition of “vacation sitting day”, for the Table substitute the
Table in schedule 3.

CJM SUTHERLAND Lord President I.P.D. Edinburgh

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Act of Sederunt)

This Act of Sederunt amends the Rules of the Court of Session 1994 to set out
the public holidays, vacation periods and vacation sitting days of the Court
of Session up to the end of the 2026 winter vacation.
