## Social Security (Special Rules for End of Life) Act 2022

      B e it enacted  by the King’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the
advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this
present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—

### Rules to apply where death expected within 12 months

1

1.1

In section 66 of the Social Security Contributions and Benefits Act 1992
(rules for attendance allowance in cases where death can reasonably be
expected within 6 months), in subsection (2)(a) (meaning of “ terminally ill ”
in relation to attendance allowance and disability living allowance), for “6”
substitute  “12” .

1.2

In section 82 of the Welfare Reform Act 2012 (rules for personal independence
payment in cases where death can reasonably be expected within 6 months), in
subsection (4) (meaning of “ terminally ill ”), for “6” substitute  “12” .

1.3

In the Universal Credit, Personal Independence Payment, Jobseeker’s Allowance
and Employment and Support Allowance (Decisions and Appeals) Regulations 2013
( S.I. 2013/381 ), in regulation 2, in the definition of “terminally ill”, for
the words from “expected” to the end substitute  “expected within 12 months” .

### Extent, commencement and short title

2

2.1

This Act extends to England and Wales and Scotland.

2.2

Section 1  comes into force on such day or days as the Secretary of State may
by regulations made by statutory instrument appoint.

2.3

Regulations under  subsection (2)  may contain such transitional, transitory
or saving provision as the Secretary of State considers appropriate.

2.4

This section comes into force on the day on which this Act is passed.

2.5

This Act may be cited as the Social Security (Special Rules for End of Life)
Act 2022.
