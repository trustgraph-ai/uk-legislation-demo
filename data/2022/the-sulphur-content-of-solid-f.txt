Environmental Protection

authority  , makes these regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by
sections 11 of, and paragraph 1(1) of Schedule 2 and paragraph 21 of Schedule

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Regulations)

These Regulations are made in exercise of the powers conferred by section 11
of, and paragraph 1(1) of Schedule 2 and paragraph 21 of Schedule 7 to, the
European Union (Withdrawal) Act 2018. The Regulations make amendments to the
legislation in the field of air quality to remedy any failure of retained EU
law to operate effectively.

Regulation 2 makes technical updating amendments to the Sulphur Content of
Solid Fuel Regulation (Northern Ireland) 1998 by substituting a new British
Standard for the determination of the total sulphur content.
