## The Licensing (Form of Licence) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Licensing

  Made   24th April 2023

  Coming into operation   31st May 2023

      The Department for Communities  makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred on it by Article 33 of the Licensing
(Northern Ireland) Order 1996  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Licensing (Form of Licence) (Amendment)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and come into operation on 31st May 2023.

### Amendment to the Licensing (Form of Licence) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1997

2

2.1

The Licensing (Form of Licence) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1997  are
amended in accordance with paragraphs (2) to (6).

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations amend the Licensing (Form of Licence) Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 1997, to take account of changes required to the form of
licence as a result of provisions within the Licensing and Registration of
Clubs (Amendment) Act (Northern Ireland) 2021. The relevant provisions
authorise underage functions in specified licensed premises, further later
opening for public houses and hotels, and the retail sale and consumption of
intoxicating liquor in a local producer’s premises under the Licensing
(Northern Ireland) Order 1996.

  These Regulations also remove references relating to provisions which
removed additional restrictions on Good Friday, Easter Day and late night
permitted hours on a Sunday.
