## The Fair Employment (Specification of Public Authorities) (Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Fair Employment

  Made   29th November 2023

  Coming into operation   1st January 2024

      The Executive Office   makes the following Order in exercise of the
powers conferred by Articles 50 and 51 of the Fair Employment and Treatment
(Northern Ireland) Order 1998  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

This Order may be cited as the Fair Employment (Specification of Public
Authorities) (Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into
operation on 1st January 2024.

### Amendment of the Fair Employment (Specification of Public Authorities) Order (Northern Ireland) 2004

2

For the Schedule to the Fair Employment (Specification of Public Authorities)
Order (Northern Ireland) 2004  substitute the Schedule set out in the Schedule
to this Order.

### Revocation

3

The Fair Employment (Specification of Public Authorities) (Amendment) Order
(Northern Ireland) 2016  is revoked.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Executive Office on 29th of
November 2023.       (L.S.)   Siobhan Broderick   A senior officer of the
Executive Office

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

This Order amends the Fair Employment (Specification of Public Authorities)
Order (Northern Ireland) 2004 (“ the 2004 Order ”) which specifies a number of
persons or bodies as public authorities for certain purposes under Part VII of
the Fair Employment and Treatment (Northern Ireland) Order 1998 (“ the 1998
Order ”).

The Schedule to the 2004 Order lists in Part I the bodies who are public
authorities for the purposes of Articles 52 to 65 of the 1998 Order and in
Part II the bodies who are public authorities for the purposes of Articles 62
to 65 of the 1998 Order.

Article 2 of this Order substitutes a new Schedule to the 2004 Order.

The following bodies have been added to Part I of the Schedule:

Dalriada Water Limited

Information Commissioner’s Office

NI Water Clear Limited

Northern Ireland Water Alpha Limited
