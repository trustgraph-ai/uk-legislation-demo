## The Student Fees (Inflation Index) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Education

  Made   20th September 2023

  Coming into operation   13th October 2023

      The Department for the Economy , in exercise of the powers conferred by
Article 4 (12) of the Higher Education (Northern Ireland) Order 2005 (“ the
2005 Order ”)  makes the following Regulations.

### Citation and commencement


1

These Regulations may be cited as The Student Fees (Inflation Index)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and come into operation on 13th October
2023.

### Inflation Index


2

The index of prices to which the Department is required by Articles
4(11)(a)(i) and (b)(i) of the 2005 Order to have regard in satisfying itself
that any increase to the basic and higher amounts is no greater than is
required to maintain the value of the amount in real terms, is the All Items
Retail Prices Index Excluding Mortgage Interest Repayments published by the
Office for Budget Responsibility.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for the Economy on 20th
September 2023.         Mark Lee   A senior officer of the Department for the
Economy

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  Article 4(12) of the 2005 Order enables the Department to specify an index
of prices to which it must have regard in deciding whether any increase in the
basic and higher tuition fee amounts is no greater than required to maintain
the value of the amounts in real terms as specified under.

  These Regulations set out that index.

  The index is published by the Office for Budget Responsibility. The basic
and higher tuition fee amounts are prescribed by the Student Fees (Amounts)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2005  S.R.2005 No.290 .

  A Section 75 assessment is not required as there is no change to existing
policy.
