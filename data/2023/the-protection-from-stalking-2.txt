## The Protection from Stalking (2022 Act) (Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Justice

  Made   9th October 2023

      The Department of Justice, in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 22 of the Protection from Stalking Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 , makes
the following Order.

### Citation


1

This Order may be cited as the Protection from Stalking (2022 Act)
(Commencement) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023.

### Appointed day


2

The day appointed for the coming into operation of sections 6 to 17 of the
Protection from Stalking Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 is 19 th  October 2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 9th
October 2023             Richard Pengelly CB   A senior officer of the
Department of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order brings sections 6 to 17 of the Protection from Stalking Act
(Northern Ireland0 2022 (“ the Act ”) into operation on 19 th  October 2023.

  Section 6 to 16 of the Act makes provision from Stalking Protection Orders
to provide protection to a person at risk of stalking.

  They will enable early police intervention pre-conviction, to address
stalking behaviours before they become entrenched, or escalate n severity and
to protect victims from more serious harm.

  Section 17 allows the Department to publish guidance on the operation of
Stalking Protection Orders.
