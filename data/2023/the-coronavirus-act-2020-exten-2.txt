## The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 (expired—not approved)

Order laid before the Assembly under section 96(2) of the Coronavirus Act
2020, for approval by resolution of the Assembly before the expiration of 40
days beginning with the day on which the Order is made

Public Health

Made 21st September 2023

Laid before the Assembly 21st September 2023

Coming into operation 24th September 2023

The Department of Health in exercise of the powers conferred by section 90(2)
of the Coronavirus Act 2020 , makes the following order.

### Citation and commencement


1

This order may be cited as The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to
Act for the Protection of Public Health) (No. 2) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023
and shall come into operation on 24th September 2023.

### Alteration of expiry date


2

Section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the Coronavirus Act 2020 do not expire at
the time when they would otherwise expire and expire instead on 24th March
2024.

Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Health on 21st September
2023 (L.S.) Peter May A senior officer of the Department of Health

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the Order)

This order extends the expiry date of section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the
Coronavirus Act 2020 to 24th March 2024.
