## The Taxi Licensing (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Road Traffic And Vehicles

  Made   5th September 2023

  Coming into operation   1st October 2023

      The Department for Infrastructure  makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by sections 13(5), 30(1) and 56(1) of the
Taxis Act (Northern Ireland) 2008  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Taxi Licensing (Amendment) Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 1st October 2023.

### Amendment of the Taxi Licensing Regulations

2

In the Taxi Licensing Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015  for Schedule 1
substitute—

  Fees for Taxi Licences

Regulations 3, 9(2) and 13

      Column (1) Matter Column (2) Fees   Application for a taxi licence
£147.00 Further application for a taxi licence within 21 days £29.50 New taxi
licence, plate or sign to replace one lost, accidentally defaced or destroyed
£10.00

.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Infrastructure on
5th September 2023         Chris Hughes   A senior officer of the   Department
for Infrastructure

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  These Regulations amend Schedule 1 to the Taxi Licensing Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 2015 to increase fees payable as follows—     1
Application for a taxi licence fee increased from £138.50 to £147.00; and
2     Further application for a taxi licence within 21 days increased from
£19.50 to £29.50.

produced and are available from Safe and Accessible Travel Division,
