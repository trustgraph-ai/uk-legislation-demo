## The Police Service of Northern Ireland (Amendment) Regulations 2023

    Police

  Made   4th April 2023

  Coming into operation   28th April 2023

      The Department of Justice, in exercise of the powers conferred by
sections 25(1) and 26(1) of the Police (Northern Ireland) Act 1998 , makes the
following Regulations. Those powers are now vested in the Department .
In accordance with sections 25(8) and 26(6) of that Act the Department has
consulted the Northern Ireland Policing Board and the Police Association for
Northern Ireland before making these Regulations.

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Police Service of Northern Ireland
(Amendment) Regulations 2023 and shall come into operation on 28th April 2023.

### Amendment of the Police Service of Northern Ireland Regulations 2005

2

2.1

The Police Service of Northern Ireland Regulations 2005  shall be amended in
accordance with paragraph (2).

2.2

In regulation 3(1) in paragraph (a) of the definition of “public holiday”, for
2023 only, after “first Monday in May” insert  “, second Monday in May” .

### Amendment of the Police Service of Northern Ireland and Police Service of Northern Ireland Reserve (Injury Benefit) Regulations 2006

3

3.1

The Police Service of Northern Ireland and Police Service of Northern Ireland
Reserve (Injury Benefit) Regulations 2006  shall be amended in accordance with
paragraph (2).

3.2

In Schedule 1 in the definition of “public holiday”, for 2023 only, after
“first Monday in May” insert  “, second Monday in May” .

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 4th April
2023         Richard Pengelly   A senior officer of the Department of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations amend the Police Service of Northern Ireland Regulations
2005 and the Police Service of Northern Ireland and Police Service of Northern
Ireland Reserve (Injury Benefit) Regulations 2006 to allow for the additional
public holiday on 8th May 2023, to mark the coronation of His Majesty King
Charles III.
