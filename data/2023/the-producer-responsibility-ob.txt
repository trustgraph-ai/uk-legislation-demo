    Environmental Protection

  Made   18th  December 2023

      The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs makes these
regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 50 of, and
paragraphs 1(1) and 2(2) of Schedule 4 to, the Environment Act 2021 (“ the Act
”)  and now vested in it .           The Department of Agriculture,
Environment and Rural Affairs makes these Regulations after consultation in
accordance with paragraph 8 of Schedule 4 to the Act and is satisfied as to
the matters specified in paragraph 9(1) of that Schedule.

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of these Regulations)

  These Regulations amend the Producer Responsibility Obligations (Packaging
Waste) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2007 ( S.I. 2007 No. 198 ) (the “ 2007
Regulations ”). Those Regulations impose on producers obligations to recycle
packaging waste in order to attain overall recycling and material-specific
recycling targets.

  These Regulations update the formula used in the calculation of the amount
of glass packaging waste to be recycled by re-melt.
