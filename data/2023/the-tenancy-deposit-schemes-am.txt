## The Tenancy Deposit Schemes (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

Landlord And Tenant

Made 16th February 2023

Coming into operation 1st April 2023

The Department for Communities  makes the following Regulations in exercise of
the powers conferred on it by Article 5B(5) and (6) and Article 72(1) of the
Private Tenancies (Northern Ireland) Order 2006 .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Tenancy Deposit Schemes (Amendment)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and come into operation on 1st April 2023.

### Amendment of the Tenancy Deposit Schemes Regulations (Northern Ireland)

2

The Tenancy Deposit Schemes Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2012  are amended
in accordance with regulation 3.

3

3.1

In regulation 12 (requirement on landlord to supply information) –

3.1.a

In paragraph (a), for “14 days” substitute  “28 days” ; and

3.1.b

In paragraph (b) –

3.1.b.i

For “28 days” substitute  “35 days” ;

3.1.b.ii

At the end insert  “and any relevant person” .

3.2

In regulation 37(2) (duty to produce an information leaflet), for “28 days”
substitute  “35 days” .

3.3

In Schedule 1 (the prescribed information to be provided when the tenancy
deposit is protected in a tenancy deposit scheme) –

3.3.a

In paragraph 1, omit “within 14 days from the receipt of the deposit from the
tenant”;

3.3.b

In paragraph 3, omit “within 28 days from the receipt of the deposit”.

Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Communities on 16th
February 2023 (L.S.) David Polley A senior officer of the Department for
Communities

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

(This note is not part of the regulations)

These Regulations amend regulation 12 paragraphs (a) and (b), regulation 37(2)
and Schedule 1, paragraphs (1) and (3) of the Tenancy Deposit Schemes
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2012 by increasing the time limits for
requirements relating to tenancy deposits.
