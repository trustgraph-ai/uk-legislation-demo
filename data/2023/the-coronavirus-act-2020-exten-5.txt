## The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to Act for the Protection of Public Health) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

  Order laid before the Assembly under section 96(2) of the Coronavirus Act
2020, for approval by resolution of the Assembly before the expiration of 40
days beginning with the day on which the Order is made

    Public Health

  Made   23rd March 2023

  Laid before the Assembly   23rd March 2023

  Coming into operation   24th March 2023

      The Department of Health , in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 90(2) of the Coronavirus Act 2020 , makes the following order.

### Citation and commencement

1

This order may be cited as The Coronavirus Act 2020 (Extension of Powers to
Act for the Protection of Public Health) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and
shall come into operation on 24th March 2023.

### Alteration of expiry date

2

Section 48 of, and Schedule 18 to, the Coronavirus Act 2020 do not expire at
the time when they would otherwise expire and expire instead on 24th September
2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Health on 23rd March
2023       (L.S.)   Peter May   A senior officer of the Department of Health
