Title: The Whole of Government Accounts (Designation of Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023
Date: 2024-04-03
Description: This Order designates bodies, for the financial year ending with 31st March 2023, for the purposes of section 15 of the Government Resources and Accounts Act (Northern Ireland) 2001. The Department of Finance (“the Department”) intends to pass the information provided by bodies so designated in the Schedule to this Order to HM Treasury for use in the preparation of the Whole of Government Accounts (WGA). The designation enables the Department to require those bodies to provide financial information in relation to the year in question. The bodies designated include the District Councils for the 11 Local Government Districts established pursuant to the Local Government (Boundaries) Act (Northern Ireland) 2008.

## The Whole of Government Accounts (Designation of Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Government Resources And Accounts

  Made   6th June 2023

  Coming into operation   28th June 2023

      The Department of Finance  , in exercise of the powers conferred by
section 15(1) of the Government Resources and Accounts Act (Northern Ireland)
2001  (‘the Act’), makes the following Order.       The bodies listed in the
Schedule to this Order appear to the Department of Finance (“ the Department
”) –     a     to exercise functions of a public nature; or         b     to
be entirely or substantially funded from public money.               The
Department intends the accounts under section 14 of the Act for the financial
year ending with 31st March 2023 to relate in part to those bodies.

### Title and commencement

1

This Order may be cited as the Whole of Government Accounts (Designation of
Bodies) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 28th
June 2023.

### Designation of Bodies

2

For the purposes of section 15 of the Government Resources and Accounts Act
(Northern Ireland) 2001, the Department designates the bodies listed in the
Schedule in respect of the financial year ending with 31st March 2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Finance on 6th June
2023.         Stuart Stevenson   A senior officer of the Department of Finance

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order designates bodies, for the financial year ending with 31st March
2023, for the purposes of section 15 of the Government Resources and Accounts
Act (Northern Ireland) 2001. The Department of Finance (“ the Department ”)
intends to pass the information provided by bodies so designated in the
Schedule to this Order to HM Treasury for use in the preparation of the Whole
of Government Accounts (WGA). The designation enables the Department to
require those bodies to provide financial information in relation to the year
in question. The bodies designated include the District Councils for the 11
Local Government Districts established pursuant to the Local Government
(Boundaries) Act (Northern Ireland) 2008.

  WGA are financial statements prepared on the basis of Generally Accepted
Accounting Practice which will cover the whole of the public sector as far as
practically possible.
