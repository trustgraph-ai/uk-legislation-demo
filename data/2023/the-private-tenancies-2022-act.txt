## The Private Tenancies (2022 Act) (Commencement No.1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Private Tenancies

  Made   16th February 2023

  Coming into operation   1st April 2023

      The Department for Communities makes the following Order in exercise of
the powers conferred on it by sections 14 (7) of the Private Tenancies Act
(Northern Ireland) 2022. .

### Citation

1

This Order may be cited as the Private Tenancies (2022 Act) (Commencement
No.1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023.

### Appointed Day

2

The day appointed for the coming into operation of the provisions of the
Private Tenancies Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 specified in the Schedule is 1st
April 2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Communities on 16th
February 2023       (L.S.)   David Polley   A senior officer of the Department
for Communities

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order brings into operation certain provisions of the Private Tenancies
Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 which make amendments to the Private Tenancies
(Northern Ireland) Order 2006. These provisions relate to tenants being given
notice of certain matters relating to a tenancy including past matters and
being given receipts for any cash payment made in relation to a tenancy. The
Order also brings into operation provisions relating to tenancy deposits. It
provides that the amount of a tenancy deposit shall be no more than one
month’s rent. In addition, it increases the time limits for requirements
relating to tenancy deposits and provides that certain offences in connection
with tenancy deposits shall be continuing offences.
