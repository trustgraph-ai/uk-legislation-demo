## The Taxi Drivers’ Licences (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Road Traffic And Vehicles

  Made   5th September 2023

  Coming into operation   1st October 2023

      The Department for Infrastructure  makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by sections 23(5), 30(1) and 56(1) of the
Taxis Act (Northern Ireland) 2008  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Taxi Drivers’ Licences (Amendment)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 1 st
October 2023.

### Amendment of the Taxi Drivers’ Licences Regulations

2

In regulation 13 of the Taxi Drivers’ Licences Regulations (Northern Ireland)
2014  (fee for a practical test) —

2.a

in sub-paragraph (a) for “£60.00” substitute  “£70.00” ; and

2.b

in sub-paragraph (b) for “£90.00” substitute  “£109.00” .

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Infrastructure on 5
th  September 2023         Chris Hughes   A senior officer of the   Department
for Infrastructure

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations amend the Taxi Drivers’ Licences Regulations (Northern
Ireland) 2014 in order to increase the fee for a practical test of competence
to drive a taxi from £60.00 to £70.00 where a test commences at any time
between 8.30am and 4.30pm during the days Monday to Friday and from £90.00 to
£109.00 where a test commences at any other time.

  An Explanatory Memorandum has been produced and is available from Safe and
Accessible Travel Division, Department for Infrastructure, Clarence Court,
10-18 Adelaide Street, Town Parks, Belfast BT2 8GB or online alongside this
Regulation at  http://www.legislation.gov.uk/nisr
