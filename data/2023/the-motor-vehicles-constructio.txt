## The Motor Vehicles (Construction and Use) (Amendment No. 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Road Traffic And Vehicles

  Made   23rd November 2023

  Coming into operation   8th January 2024

      The Department for Infrastructure  makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by Articles 55(1) and 110(2) of the Road
Traffic (Northern Ireland) Order 1995  and now vested in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Motor Vehicles (Construction and Use)
(Amendment No. 2) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into
operation on 8th January 2024.

### Amendment of the Motor Vehicles (Construction and Use) Regulations

2

In regulation 125A of the Motor Vehicles (Construction and Use) Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 1999  (mobile telephones)—

2.a

in paragraphs (1) to (3) for “paragraph (5)” substitute  “paragraphs (5) and
(5A)” ; and

2.b

after paragraph (5) insert—

2.b.5A

A person does not contravene a provision of this regulation if, at the time of
the alleged contravention—

2.b.5A.a

he is using the telephone or other device only to perform a remote controlled
parking function of the motor vehicle; and

2.b.5A.b

that telephone or other device only enables the motor vehicle to move where
the following conditions are satisfied—

2.b.5A.b.i

there is continuous activation of the remote control application of the
telephone or device by the driver;

2.b.5A.b.ii

the signal between the motor vehicle and the telephone or the motor vehicle
and the device, as the case may be, is maintained; and

2.b.5A.b.iii

the distance between the motor vehicle and the telephone or the motor vehicle
and the device, as the case may be, is not more than 6 metres.

.

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations amend the Motor Vehicles (Construction and Use)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 1999 (“ the Construction and Use Regulations ”)
to facilitate the introduction of remote control parking systems.

  They insert an additional exemption into regulation 125A of the Construction
and Use Regulations to provide that a person is not in contravention of that
regulation when using a hand-held mobile telephone or other hand-held
communication device to perform a remote control parking manoeuvre so long as
the telephone or device is continuously activated by the driver, the signal
between the motor vehicle and the telephone or device is maintained and the
distance from the motor vehicle is not more than 6 metres.
