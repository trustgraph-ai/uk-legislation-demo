## The Motor Vehicle Testing (Amendment) (Fees) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Road Traffic And Vehicles

  Made   5th September 2023

  Coming into operation   1st October 2023

      The Department for Infrastructure  makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by Articles 61(6), 62, 75(8), 81(8) and (9)
and 110(2) of the Road Traffic (Northern Ireland) Order 1995  and now vested
in it .

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Motor Vehicle Testing (Amendment) (Fees)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 1st
October 2023.

### Amendment of the Motor Vehicle Testing Regulations

2

For Schedule 1 to the Motor Vehicle Testing Regulations (Northern Ireland)
2003 , substitute—

  FEES

Regulations 7(2), 11(3), (4) and (5), 16(1), 18(2) and 22

      1. Fee payable for examination under regulation 7(2) or 11(3) or 16(1)
(a) Vehicle in Class I or II £34.00 (b) Vehicle in Class III or IV £38.00 (c)
Vehicle in Class V £70.50 (d) Vehicle in Class VA— (i)  with 13 to 16 seats
exclusive of driver  £82.50 (ii) with 17 to 35 seats exclusive of driver
£94.00 (iii) with more than 35 seats exclusive of driver £110.00 (e) Vehicle
in Class VI £70.50 (f) Vehicle in Class VIA £82.50 2. Fee payable for re-
examination under regulation 11(4) (a) Vehicle in Class I or II £29.50 (b)
Vehicle in Class III or IV £29.50 (c) Vehicle in Class V £38.00 (d) Vehicle in
Class VA— (i)  with 13 to 16 seats exclusive of driver  £38.00 (ii) with 17 to
35 seats exclusive of driver £38.00 (iii) with more than 35 seats exclusive of
driver £38.00 (e) Vehicle in Class VI £38.00 (f) Vehicle in Class VIA £38.00
3. Fee payable for re-examination under regulation 11(5) (a) Vehicle in Class
VA— (i)  with 13 to 16 seats exclusive of driver  £38.00 (ii) with 17 to 35
seats exclusive of driver £38.00 (iii) with more than 35 seats exclusive of
driver £38.00 (b) Vehicle in Class VIA £38.00 4. Fee payable for a full
examination under Article 81 of the Order (a) Vehicle in Class I or II £34.00
(b) Vehicle in Class III or IV £38.00 (c) Vehicle in Class V £70.50 (d)
Vehicle in Class VA— (i)  with 13 to 16 seats exclusive of driver  £82.50 (ii)
with 17 to 35 seats exclusive of driver £94.00 (iii) with more 35 seats
exclusive of driver £110.00 (e) Vehicle in Class VI £70.50 (f) Vehicle in
Class VIA £82.50 (g) Public service vehicle (not being a taxi) £102.50 (h)
Taxi £147.00 (i) Goods vehicle of 3,500 kg maximum gross weight or under
£38.00 (j) Goods vehicle over 3,500 kg maximum gross weight (i)  2 axle rigid
vehicle  £101.00 (ii) 3 axle rigid vehicle £109.00 (iii) 4 axle rigid vehicle

and appeals under regulations 7(2), 11(3) and 16(1)—     a     for vehicles in
Classes I or II from £22.00 to £34.00;         b     for vehicles in Classes
III or IV from £30.50 to £38.00;         c     for vehicles in Class V, from

vehicles in Class VIA, from £53.50 to £82.50.             2     Re-examination
