## The Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit) (Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Housing

  Made   16th December 2022

  Coming into operation   1st February 2023

      The Department for Communities , in exercise of the powers conferred by
Article 64 and 148(1) of the Housing (Northern Ireland) Order 2003  hereby
makes the following Order:

### Citation and commencement

1

This Order may be cited as the Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit)
(Amendment) Order (Northern Ireland) 2023 and shall come into operation on 1st
February 2023.

### Amendment of the Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2003

2

2.1

Article 2 (Maximum Amount) of the Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit)
Order (Northern Ireland) 2003  is amended in accordance with paragraphs (2)
and (3).

2.2

In paragraph (1)(b), for “£25,000” substitute  “£35,000” .

2.3

In paragraph (2) –

2.3.a

For “£25,000” substitute  “£35,000” ,

2.3.b

For “£50,000” substitute  “£70,000” .

### Saving Provision

3

This Order shall not have effect in relation to an application for a grant
made before 1st February 2023.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department for Communities on 16th
December 2022       (L.S.)   Paul Price   A senior official of the Department
for Communities

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  The Housing Renovation etc. Grants (Grant Limit) Order (Northern Ireland)
2003 makes provision in relation to the amount of housing grants that the
Housing Executive may pay.

  These Regulations amend Articles 2 and 3 of the Housing Renovation etc.
Grants (Grant Limit) Order (Northern Ireland) 2003 enabling the Housing
Executive to pay a higher maximum amount of mandatory disabled facilities
grant.
