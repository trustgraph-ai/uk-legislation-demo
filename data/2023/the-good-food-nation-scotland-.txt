## The Good Food Nation (Scotland) Act 2022 (Commencement No. 1) Regulations 2023

    Food

  Made   15th June 2023

  Laid before the Scottish Parliament   16th June 2023

  Coming into force   30th June 2023

      The Scottish Ministers make the following Regulations in exercise of the
powers conferred by section 28(2) of the Good Food Nation (Scotland) Act 2022
.

### Citation, commencement, and interpretation

1

1.1

These Regulations may be cited as the Good Food Nation (Scotland) Act 2022
(Commencement No. 1) Regulations 2023 and come into force on 30 June 2023.

1.2

In these Regulations, “ the Act ” means the Good Food Nation (Scotland) Act
2022.

### Appointed days

2

30 June 2023 is the day appointed for the coming into force of the following
provisions of the Act—

2.a

section 1 (requirement to produce plan),

2.b

section 2 (preparation of plan: principles),

2.c

section 4 (preparation of plan: consultation),

2.d

section 5 (preparation of plan: consideration of international instruments),

2.e

section 9 (publication of documents: inclusive communications).

3

  30 June 2024   is the day appointed for the coming into force of the
following provisions of the Act—

3.a

section 3 (requirement to lay proposed plan before the parliament),

3.b

section 7 (reporting),

3.c

section 8 (review and revision of plan).

SHONA ROBISON A member of the Scottish Government St Andrew’s House, Edinburgh

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations bring sections 1, 2, 4, 5 and 9 of the Good Food Nation
(Scotland) Act 2022 (“ the Act ”) into force on 30 June 2023. These
Regulations bring section 3, 7 and 8 of the Act into force on 31 December
2023.

  The Bill for the Act received Royal Assent on 26 July 2022. Sections 24, 25,
27, 28 and 29 came into force on the following day.
