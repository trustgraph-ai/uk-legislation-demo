## The Marine Protected Areas (Prohibited Methods of Fishing) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023

    Sea Fisheries

  Made   15th September 2023

  Coming into operation   15th October 2023

      The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs  in
exercise of the powers conferred by sections 19(1) and 124(1), (2) and (2A) of
the Fisheries Act (Northern Ireland) 1966 , makes the following Regulations.

### Citation and commencement


1

These Regulations may be cited as the Marine Protected Areas (Prohibited
Methods of Fishing) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023 and come
into operation on 15th October 2023.

### Amendment of the Marine Protected Areas (Prohibited Methods of Fishing) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2022


2

2.1

The Marine Protected Areas (Prohibited Methods of Fishing) Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 2022  are amended as follows.

2.2

In Schedule 8 (The Maidens Special Area of Conservation) in the table (Area
3)—

2.2.a

in the longitude coordinate at Point B for “005° 42.010’W” substitute  “005°
45.010’W” ;

2.2.b

in the longitude coordinate at Point C for “005° 43.250’W” substitute  “005°
42.250’W” .

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Agriculture,
Environment and Rural Affairs on 15th September 2023.         Owen Lyttle   A
senior officer of the   Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural
Affairs

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations make minor corrections to two coordinates which provide
for the Maidens Special Area of Conservation in Schedule 8 to the Marine
Protected Areas (Prohibited Methods of Fishing) Regulations (Northern Ireland)
2022.
