## The Period Products (Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs Specified Public Service Bodies) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024

    Period Products (free Provision)

Laid before the Assembly in draft

  Made   16th April 2024

  Coming into operation   16th April 2024

      The Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs  makes the
following Regulations in exercise of the powers conferred by section 2(1) and
(13) of the Period Products (Free Provision) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 . In
accordance with section 2(8)(a) of that Act, the Department of Agriculture,
Environment and Rural Affairs has consulted with the public service bodies
specified by these Regulations and such other bodies or persons as it thinks
appropriate.

### Citation and Commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Period Products (Department of
Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs Specified Public Service Bodies)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on 16th
April 2024.

### Specified public service bodies

2

The bodies listed in the Schedule to these Regulation are specified as public
service bodies to which the duty in section 2(3) of the Period Products (Free
Provision) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022 applies.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Agriculture,
Environment and Rural Affairs on 16th April 2024.         Sharon McFlynn   A
senior officer of the   Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural
Affairs

  Specified Public Service Bodies

Regulation 2

  The Agri-Food and Biosciences Institute, as established by the Agriculture
(Northern Ireland) Order 2004 ;

  The Livestock and Meat Commission for Northern Ireland, as established by
the Livestock Marketing Commission Act (Northern Ireland) 1967 ; and

  The Northern Ireland Fishery Harbour Authority, as established by the
Northern Ireland Fishery Harbour Authority Order (Northern Ireland) 1973 .

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations specify the public service bodies within the functions of
the Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs that are subject
to the duty to ensure that period products are obtainable free of charge by
persons on their premises who need to use them from a date specified in the
Period Products (Free Provision) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022. The following
bodies are designated specified public service bodies: the Agri-Food and
Biosciences Institute; the Livestock and Meat Commission for Northern Ireland;
and the Northern Ireland Fishery Harbour Authority.
