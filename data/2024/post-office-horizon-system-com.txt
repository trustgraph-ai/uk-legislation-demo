## Post Office (Horizon System) Compensation Act 2024

      B e it enacted  by the King’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the
advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this
present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—

### Expenditure in connection with compensation schemes relating to Post Office Horizon system etc.

1

1.1

There may be paid out of money provided by Parliament expenditure incurred by
the Secretary of State under, or in connection with, schemes or other
arrangements—

1.1.a

to compensate persons affected by the Horizon system;

1.1.b

to compensate persons in respect of other matters identified in High Court
judgments given in proceedings relating to the Horizon system.

1.2

“ The Horizon system ” means previous versions of the computer system known as
Horizon (and sometimes referred to as Legacy Horizon, Horizon Online or HNG-X)
used by Post Office Limited.

### Short title

2

This Act may be cited as the Post Office (Horizon System) Compensation Act
2024.
