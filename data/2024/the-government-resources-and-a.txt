## The Government Resources and Accounts (Northern Ireland) Act 2001 (Estimates and Accounts) (Designation of Bodies) Order 2024

    Government Resources And Accounts

  Made   16th May 2024

  Coming into operation   6th June 2024

      The Department of Finance  , in exercise of the powers conferred by
sections 8A and 8B of the Government Resources and Accounts Act (Northern
Ireland) 2001  , makes the following Order.           In accordance with
section 8A(5) of the Act the Department of Finance have consulted, where they
think it appropriate, with the Treasury.

### Title and commencement

1

This Order may be cited as the Government Resources and Accounts (Northern
Ireland) Act 2001 (Estimates and Accounts) (Designation of Bodies) Order 2024
and shall come into operation on 6 th  June 2024.

### Amendments to the principal Order

2

A body which is listed in a Part of the Schedule to this Order is a designated
body for the purposes of sections 8A and 8B of the Government Resources and
Accounts (Northern Ireland) Act 2001—

2.a

in relation to the department named in the heading to that Part, and

2.b

for the financial year which ends on 31st March 2025.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Finance on 16 th  May
2024         Barry Armstrong   A senior officer of the   Department of Finance

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  This Order designates specified central government bodies in relation to
named government departments for the purpose of this departments’ supply
estimates and resource accounts.

  The designations have effect for the financial year that ends on 31 st
March 2025.

  An impact assessment has not been prepared for this instrument as no impact
on the private or voluntary sector is foreseen. An Explanatory Memorandum is
available alongside the instrument on  www.legislation.gov.uk .
