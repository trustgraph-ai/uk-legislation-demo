## The Pensions (2005 Order) (Codes of Practice) (Revocation) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024

    Pensions

  Made   13 th June 2024

  Coming into operation   5th July 2024

      The Department for Communities  makes the following Order in exercise of
the powers conferred by Article 87(1) of the Pensions (Northern Ireland) Order
2005 .           The Pensions Regulator consents to the making of this Order
as required by Article 87(2) of the Pensions (Northern Ireland) Order 2005.

### Citation and commencement

1

This Order may be cited as the Pensions (2005 Order) (Codes of Practice)
(Revocation) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024 and shall come into operation on
5th July 2024.

### Revocation of codes of practice

2

The following Codes of Practice are revoked—

2.a

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 1: Reporting breaches of the law ;

2.b

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 4: Early leavers – reasonable
periods ;

2.c

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 5: Reporting late payment of
contributions to occupational money purchase schemes ;

2.d

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 6: Reporting late payment of
contributions to personal pensions ;

2.e

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 7: Trustee knowledge and
understanding (TKU) ;

2.f

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 8: Member-nominated trustees and
directors – putting in place and implementing arrangements ;

2.g

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 9: Internal controls ;

2.h

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 11: Dispute resolution –
reasonable periods ;

2.i

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 7: Trustee knowledge and
understanding (TKU): Second issue ;

2.j

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 5: Reporting late payment of
contributions to occupational pension schemes: Second issue ;

2.k

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 6: Reporting late payment of
contributions to personal pension schemes: Second issue ;

2.l

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 13: Governance and administration
of occupational trust-based schemes ;

2.m

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 14: Governance and administration
of public service pension schemes ;

2.n

the Pensions Regulator Code of Practice No. 13: Governance and administration
of occupational trust-based schemes providing money purchase benefits: Second
issue .

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This is not part of the Order)

  This Order revokes codes of practice that were issued by the Pensions
Regulator. The Pensions Regulator has issued the General code of practice
which consolidates ten existing codes of practice and provides guidance to
those running occupational, personal and public service pension schemes.

  The General code of practice is available on the Pensions Regulator’s
website:  https://www.thepensionsregulator.gov.uk/en/document-library . Copies
may be obtained by post from the Pensions Regulator, Telecom House, 125-135
Preston Road, Brighton BN1 6AF.
