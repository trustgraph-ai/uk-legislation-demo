## Zoological Society of London (Leases) Act 2024

      B e it enacted  by the King’s most Excellent Majesty, by and with the
advice and consent of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal, and Commons, in this
present Parliament assembled, and by the authority of the same, as follows:—

### Maximum term of leases for Zoological Society of London

1

In section 7(2) of the Crown Estate Act 1961 (power of the Secretary of State
to grant leases to the Zoological Society of London in relation to certain
land in Regent’s Park), for “sixty years” substitute  “150 years” .

### Extent, commencement and short title

2

2.1

This Act extends to England and Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

2.2

This Act comes into force at the end of the period of two months beginning
with the day on which it is passed.

2.3

This Act may be cited as the Zoological Society of London (Leases) Act 2024.
