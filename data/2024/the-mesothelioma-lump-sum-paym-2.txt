## The Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (2023 Conditions and Amounts) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024

  Regulations made by the Department for Communities and laid before the
Assembly under section 10(2) of the Mesothelioma, etc., Act (Northern Ireland)
2008 for approval of the Assembly before the expiration of six months from the
date of their coming into operation

    Social Security

  Made   21st March 2024

  Coming into operation   22nd March 2024

      The Department for Communities makes the following Regulations in
exercise of the powers conferred by sections 1(3) and 9(2)(a) and (3) of the
Mesothelioma, etc., Act (Northern Ireland) 2008 .

### Citation, commencement and application

1

1.1

These Regulations may be cited as the Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (2023
Conditions and Amounts) (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024 and
shall come into operation on 22nd March 2024.

1.2

In regulation 2—

1.2.a

paragraph (a) applies only in relation to a person—

1.2.a.i

first diagnosed with diffuse mesothelioma on or after 1st April 2023; or

1.2.a.ii

where the date of the first diagnosis with diffuse mesothelioma is unknown, if
a claim under section 1(1) of the Mesothelioma, etc., Act (Northern Ireland)
2008 is made by that person on or after 1st April 2023; and

1.2.b

paragraph (b) applies only in relation to the dependant of a person who dies
on or after 1st April 2023 and who, immediately before death, had diffuse
mesothelioma.

### Amendment of the Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (Conditions and Amounts) Regulations

2

In the Schedule to the Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (Conditions and Amounts)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2008 —

2.a

for Table 1 substitute the Table set out in Part 1 of the Schedule to these
Regulations; and

2.b

for Table 2 substitute the Table set out in Part 2 of the Schedule to these
Regulations.

### Revocation

3

The Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (Conditions and Amounts) (Amendment No. 2)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2023  are revoked.

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations revoke and re-enact the provisions of the Mesothelioma
Lump Sum Payments (Conditions and Amounts) (Amendment No. 2) Regulations
(Northern Ireland) 2023 which would otherwise cease to have effect by virtue
of section 10(2) of the Mesothelioma, etc., Act (Northern Ireland) 2008. These
Regulations amend the Mesothelioma Lump Sum Payments (Conditions and Amounts)
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2008 to increase amounts of lump sums payable
to people with diffuse mesothelioma or to their dependants under the
Mesothelioma, etc., Act (Northern Ireland) 2008. The increase in each case is
10.1 per cent, rounded up or down to the nearest £1 as appropriate.
