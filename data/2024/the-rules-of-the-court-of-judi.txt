## The Rules of the Court of Judicature (Northern Ireland) (Amendment) 2024

    Court Of Judicature, Northern Ireland   Procedure

  Made   3rd October 2024

  Coming into operation   2nd December 2024

      The Northern Ireland Court of Judicature Rules Committee  makes the
following Rules in exercise of the powers conferred by sections 55 and 55A of
the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978 .

### Citation, commencement and application

1

These Rules shall be cited as the Rules of the Court of Judicature (Northern
Ireland) (Amendment) 2024 and shall come into operation on 2nd December 2024.

### Amendments to the Rules of the Court of Judicature (Northern Ireland) 1980

2

The Rules of the Court of Judicature (Northern Ireland) 1980 are amended as
follows—

2.1

in the Arrangement of Orders, after the entry relating to Order 61A, insert
“61B. Appeals to the Supreme Court in civil cases” ; and

2.2

after Order 61A, insert 61B (Appeals to the Supreme Court in civil cases) as
set out in the Schedule to these Rules.

Rule 2(2)

  ORDER 61B

######### Leave to appeal from the Court of Appeal to the Supreme Court in civil cases

1

This Order applies where leave is sought to appeal to the Supreme Court under
section 42 of the Judicature (Northern Ireland) Act 1978.

2

An application for leave to appeal shall be made to the Court of Appeal.

3

The application under Rule 2 shall be made within 28 days of the date of the
order or judgment of the Court of Appeal which the applicant wishes to appeal.

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Rules)

  These Rules amend the Rules of the Court of Judicature (Northern Ireland)
1980 ( S.R. 1980 No. 346 ) to provide that an application to the Court of
Appeal for leave to appeal to the Supreme Court in a civil case shall be made
within 28 days of the decision or order in respect of which leave is sought.
