## The Health and Social Care Pension Scheme (Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024

    Health And Personal Social Services   Public Sector Pensions

  Made   8th March 2024

  Coming into operation  - -   1st April 2024

      The Department of Health makes the following Regulations in exercise of
the powers conferred by Articles 12(1), (2) and (3) of, and (14)(1) and (2)
of, and Schedule 3 to the Superannuation (Northern Ireland) Order 1972  (“ the
1972 Order ”) and sections 1 (1) and (2)(e), 2(1) and 3(1) to (3) of, and
paragraph 5 of Schedule 2 and Schedule 3 to, the Public Service Pensions Act
(Northern Ireland) 2014 (“ the 2014 Act ”).       In accordance with Article
12(4) of the 1972 Order and section 21(1) of the 2014 Act, the Department of
Health has consulted such persons as appear to the Department of Health likely
to be affected by these Regulations.           In accordance with Article
12(1) of the 1972 Order and section 3(5) of the 2014 Act, these Regulations
are made with the consent of the Department of Finance.

### Citation and commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme
(Amendment) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024 and come into operation on 1
April 2024.

### Amendment of the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015

2

In the Health and Social Care Pension Scheme Regulations (Northern Ireland)
2015  in regulation 32 (Contributions by employing authorities), in paragraph
(1), for “22.5%” substitute  “23.2%” .

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations further amend The Health and Social Care Pension Scheme
Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2015 ( S.R. 2015 No. 120 ) (“ the 2015
Regulations ”).

  These Regulations come into operation on 1 st  April 2024.

  Regulation 1 provides for citation, commencement.

  Regulation 2 makes amendments in respect of the 2015 Regulations in that
they increase the employer contribution rate from 1 st  April 2024.
