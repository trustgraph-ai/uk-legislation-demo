## Gender Representation on Public Boards (Amendment) (Scotland) Act 2024

### Amendment of the 2018 Act

1

In section 2 (key definitions) of the Gender Representation on Public Boards
(Scotland) Act 2018, the definition of “woman” is repealed.

### Commencement

2

This Act comes into force on the day after Royal Assent.

### Short title

3

The short title of this Act is the Gender Representation on Public Boards
(Amendment) (Scotland) Act 2024.
