## The Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (Commencement No. 1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024

    Proceeds Of Crime

  Made   26 th March 2024

  Coming into operation   26 th April 2024

      The Department of Justice makes the following Order, in exercise of the
powers conferred by section 219(5) of the Economic Crime and Corporate
Transparency Act 2023 .           In accordance with section 219(5) of that
Act, the Department of Justice has consulted the Secretary of State

### Citation and interpretation

1

1.1

This Order may be cited as the Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act
2023 (Commencement No. 1) Order (Northern Ireland) 2024.

1.2

In this Order “ the 2023 Act ” means the Economic Crime and Corporate
Transparency Act 2023.

### Appointed Day

2

The day appointed for the coming into operation of Part 3 of Schedule 8 to the
2023 Act (cryptoassets: confiscation orders) and section 179 of the 2023 Act
so far as it relates to Part 3 of Schedule 8 and, so far as not already in
force, is 26th April 2024.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Department of Justice on 26th March
2024.         Naomi Long   Minister of Justice

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Order)

  This Order brings into operation on 26th April 2024 Part 3 of Schedule 8 to
the 2023 Act, and section 179 of the 2023 Act in so far as it relates to Part
3 of Schedule 8, which deals with cryptoassets in relation to confiscation
orders.
