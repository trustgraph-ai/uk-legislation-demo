## The Period Products (the Executive Office Specified Public Service Bodies) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024

    Period Products (free Provision)

Laid before the Assembly in draft

  Made   23rd October 2024

  Coming into operation   25th October 2024

      The Executive Office makes the following Regulations in exercise of the
powers conferred by section 2(1) and (13) of the Period Products (Free
Provision) Act (Northern Ireland) 2022  . In accordance with section 2(8)(a)
of that Act it has consulted with the public service bodies specified by these
Regulations.

### Citation and Commencement

1

These Regulations may be cited as the Period Products (the Executive Office
Specified Public Service Bodies) Regulations (Northern Ireland) 2024 and shall
come into operation on 25 h  October 2024.

### Specified public service bodies

2

The bodies listed in the Schedule are specified as public service bodies to
which the duty in section 2(3) of the Period Products (Free Provision) Act
(Northern Ireland) 2022 applies.

      Sealed with the Official Seal of the Executive Office on 23 rd  October
2024         Richard Irwin   A senior officer of the   Department

  Specified Public Service Bodies

Regulation 2

  The Commissioner for Survivors of Institutional Childhood Abuse established
by section 21 of the Historical Institutional Abuse (Northern Ireland) Act
2019  ;

  The Commission for Victims and Survivors for Northern Ireland established by
Article 4 of the Victims and Survivors (Northern Ireland) Order 2006  ;

  The Equality Commission for Northern Ireland established by section 73 of
the Northern Ireland Act 1998  ;

  The Historical Institutional Abuse Redress Board established by section 1 of
the Historical Institutional Abuse (Northern Ireland) Act 2019;

  The Maze / Long Kesh Development Corporation established by Article 3 of the
Strategic Investment and Regeneration of Sites (Maze/Long Kesh Development
Corporation) Order (Northern Ireland) 2011  ;

  The Northern Ireland Judicial Appointments Commission established by section
3 of the Justice (Northern Ireland) Act 2002  ;

  The Strategic Investment Board Limited designated for the purposes of Part
II of the Strategic Investment and Regeneration of Sites Order (Northern
Ireland) 2003   by the Strategic Investment and Regeneration of Sites
(Designation) Order (Northern Ireland) 2003  ; and

  The Victims’ Payments Board established by regulation 3 of the Victims’
Payments Regulations 2020

## EXPLANATORY NOTE

    (This note is not part of the Regulations)

  These Regulations specify the public service bodies within the functions of
the Executive Office that are subject to the duty to ensure that period
products are obtainable free of charge by persons on their premises who need
to use them.
